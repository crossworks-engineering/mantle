# v0.141.0 — The Team Workspace: /team becomes a read-only mirror of your brain

Team members no longer land on a curated briefing page — they land in **the
workspace itself**. `/team` now looks and works like Mantle: your brain's name
and colour theme, a section nav (Notes, Pages, Tables, Apps, Tasks, Events,
Files), and a footer with your shared folders and the Assistant. Everything a
member sees derives from your **active shares** — flip "Team members only" (or
public) on any item and it appears in the matching section immediately. No hub
editing, no curation, current by construction.

## Features

- **Share anything, folders and tables included.** Tables and folders join
  pages, notes, tasks, events, files, and apps as shareable. A **table** share
  shows the last *committed* workbook — never your draft — as a read-only grid
  with tabs and lazy row paging. A **folder** share covers every file under it,
  **subfolders included, evaluated live**: drop a file in later and it's
  covered; move one out and it's gone from the share. (The files root itself
  deliberately can't be shared — "share my entire filesystem" should never be
  one accidental toggle.)
- **The member workspace.** Each section is the familiar master-detail: a card
  list on the left, the item rendered read-only on the right. Files are
  download-only. Apps open in their team frame. Members keep their own
  light/dark preference; the colour theme is your brain's brand. Same token,
  same instant revocation — remove a member and everything dies mid-session.
- **The Assistant, one tap away.** The footer's Assistant opens the team
  responder — ask anything the brain knows, every turn audited per member.
- **Shared links, at a glance.** Team admin grows a **Shared links** tab: every
  active link (public and team), with copy, open, and revoke. The exposure
  counterpart to zero-curation sharing — one screen answers "what can people
  outside see right now?"
- **The hub lives on at `/hub`.** Your designated Team Hub app (or the built-in
  briefing hub) moved from `/team` to `/hub`, unchanged. The same cookie opens
  both surfaces; existing team-shared pages appear in the workspace's Pages
  section automatically — nothing to migrate, nothing to re-share.
- **A sharper composer.** The chat box on both the owner assistant and Team
  Chat gets a taller minimum height, a thick frame, full-height buttons, and a
  brand-tinted gradient band — one shared definition, themed per brain.

## Notes

- **Deploy: tag-only bump — no migration, no compose change.**
- Audited before merge: a four-dimension adversarially-verified review (every
  finding independently re-verified against the code); nothing above low
  severity survived, and the dedicated security pass found no exploitable
  path — folder scoping, draft isolation, and team gating were each proven
  live.
- Tell your team: `/team` is the workspace now; the old hub is at `/hub`.
  Their tokens keep working — nothing to re-enter.
