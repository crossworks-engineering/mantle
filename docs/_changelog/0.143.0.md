# v0.143.0 — Find anything, start a topic from anywhere, attach files with review

The member workspace grows up: every section — and the forum — is now
searchable, sortable, and paginated, so a team surface with hundreds of shared
items or long-running threads stays navigable. Starting a conversation drops
to zero friction: type into the box on the landing page and a titled forum
topic exists before you've thought of a name — the brain writes the title for
you. And members can finally hand the team real files: forum posts take
attachments, quarantined until the brain's admin reviews them.

## Search, sort + pagination everywhere members read

- **Every workspace section** (Pages, Notes, Tables, Apps, Tasks, Events,
  Files) gets a search box, a sort menu — newest shared, oldest shared,
  recently updated, title — and a pager. Search matches titles and summaries;
  everything is URL-driven, so a filtered view is linkable and refresh-safe,
  and the back button behaves.
- **The forum topic index** searches topic titles *and post bodies* — "find
  the thread where we discussed X" works even when X never made the title.
  Sortable (latest activity, newest, oldest, title — pinned announcements
  stay on top in every order) and paginated.
- **Inside a topic**, long threads no longer truncate: **Load earlier posts**
  pages back through the full history, and a find box searches the thread —
  click a match and the view pages back to that post, scrolls to it, and
  highlights it.
- **Owner side too:** the team-admin Topics tab gets the same search (titles +
  bodies) and pagination, with your place preserved as you move between
  topics.

## Start a topic from the landing page

- The workspace landing gains a **quick composer**: one box, a Private
  switch, a "Start topic" button. No title field, no kind picker — write
  what's on your mind and you land in the new forum thread with the
  assistant's answer already streaming.
- **The brain titles the topic.** Your message is summarized into a short
  title by the brain's summarizer worker (same one behind `summarize_text`),
  with a hard timeout and a first-line fallback so topic creation never waits
  on — or fails because of — a model. Worker usage is tracked, and every
  fallback is logged so a misconfigured summarizer is visible, not silent.

## Forum file uploads, with owner review

- **Attach files to forum posts.** A paperclip on the reply composer and the
  new-topic dialog takes up to five files per post (25 MB each, with a daily
  per-member budget). Attachments render as chips — filename, size, one-click
  download — for everyone who can see the topic.
- **Nothing enters the brain unreviewed.** Uploaded bytes are held in a
  quarantine outside the knowledge tree; the assistant sees only filenames.
  The team-admin **Requests** tab gains an *Uploads awaiting review* queue,
  grouped by topic: **Download** to inspect, **Move to files** to approve —
  the file lands in `files/review/<topic>/` and only then joins the brain's
  knowledge — or **Dismiss** to drop it. The Requests badge counts waiting
  uploads, and members see an "in review" tag until you decide.
- **Safe by construction.** Every download is served with hardened headers
  (no stored-XSS via uploaded HTML/SVG), attachments bind to their post
  atomically, review actions are race-safe (a double-click can't file twice),
  and abandoned uploads are swept after 24 hours.

## Under the hood

- Thread transcripts stay **contiguous**: posts that slide out of the live
  window as new activity arrives are folded into the loaded history instead
  of leaving a silent gap.
- Search boxes adopt external URL changes (back/forward, shared links)
  instead of fighting them; pagers can't transiently disagree with their
  totals; a section that fails to refresh says so instead of going silently
  stale.
- New unit coverage for the search-snippet, auto-title, and upload-helper
  text pipelines (35 new tests); suite green at 2,361.
