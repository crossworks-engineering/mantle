# 0.120.0 — 2026-07-07

**Team Hub — /team is now a briefing site, not just a chat.** Team members
landing on **/team** get a proper welcome: a hero with their name, briefing
cards curated by you, live stats straight from the brain's memory (pages,
files, tables, journal entries…), and "Ask the brain" one tap away — the same
Team Chat as before, same token, nothing to re-learn. It's built for keeping a
whole team (including the people who just joined the project) up to date
without writing a single status email: the hub serves itself from the brain,
so it's current by construction.

Curating the hub is just sharing: open any Page, hit **Share**, and flip the
new **Team members only** toggle — the page becomes a briefing card on the
hub, in the order you shared them (share your Vision page first). Revoke the
link and it's off the hub. Team-mode links now work for every content kind,
not only apps: the page itself, its images, everything behind the link asks
for a live team credential — and a member browsing from the hub is recognised
automatically, no second token entry. Removing a member still locks them out
of all of it instantly.
