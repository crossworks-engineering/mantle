# 0.32.0 — 2026-06-23

Config — see at a glance how your brain's setup differs from the shipped template.

## Highlights

- **The Config sanity checker.** A new `/settings/config` screen compares your
  live agents, skills, tool groups, and AI workers against the shipped manifest
  template — the same source of truth onboarding and the boot reconcile use — and
  shows, per item, what's **OK**, **missing**, **modified**, or **operator-added**.
  It answers the question the integrity check couldn't: not just "does everything
  resolve?" but "*what* differs from the template, field by field." Tool-group
  membership, skill bodies, delegation wiring, and worker models are diffed
  side-by-side so a capability that never reached an existing brain — or an edit
  that has drifted from the template — is finally visible.

- **Anchored on your real responder.** The persona check measures whichever agent
  actually serves as your assistant (slug-flexible, like the integrity checker),
  and compares **structure only** — its tool groups, skills, and delegation. Your
  persona's prompt, model, and parameters are operator-owned and deliberately left
  out of the diff.

- **Read-only for now.** This release ships the visibility layer; adopting a
  change from the template per item (and deriving onboarding entirely from the
  same template) builds on this engine in a later update.

## Notes

- No schema migration in this release. The checker is read-only — it never writes
  to your config.
