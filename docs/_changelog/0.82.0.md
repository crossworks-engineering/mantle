# 0.82.0 — 2026-06-28

**Tasks and Journal.** The content surfaces formerly called Todos and Lifelogs
are now **Tasks** and **Journal** — clearer names for what they hold — and
onboarding now asks what the *brain* is for, not who you are. (Also carries the
untagged v0.80.0/v0.81.0 work.)

## Highlights

- **Todos → Tasks, Lifelogs → Journal.** Renamed end to end — navigation,
  tools, and storage (migrations 0108/0109) — with shares and peer shares
  following along. Existing data carries over unchanged.
- **Purpose onboarding.** The wizard captures the brain's purpose (an archetype
  plus free text) instead of a personal interview, and the assistant grounds
  itself in that purpose from the first turn.
- **Runners console.** `/runners` is an in-app admin console for durable
  workflow runners — list, detail, steps, queue health, plus
  cancel/resume/restart/fork (from v0.80.0).
- **Detached-client fixes.** Embedded page images and the assistant's turn
  submission now route through the API-base-aware transports.
