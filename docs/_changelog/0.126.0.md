# 0.126.0 — 2026-07-09

**Team Chat reads like the assistant chat.** The team member chat now uses the
same turn layout as the product's main chat: your question sits as a compact
card in the right margin, anchored beside the reply it produced, and the reply
renders as a full-width document — with a thin divider between exchanges, a
thinking indicator that narrates what the brain is doing, and a copy button and
timestamp on every answer.

## Highlights

- **Question beside answer.** Each exchange is one visual unit: the reply is
  the document, your question stays pinned beside it while you scroll a long
  answer (on phones it stacks question-then-answer).
- **Live progress.** While the brain works you see the same status labels the
  owner sees ("Searching the brain…", "Reading a page…"), then the answer
  streams in place.
- **Reading position respected.** Scrolling up to re-read no longer gets
  yanked to the bottom by a streaming reply — a jump-to-latest pill appears
  instead, and images settling in a reply no longer strand the scroll.
- **Small courtesies.** Full-date tooltips on timestamps, copy-to-clipboard on
  every reply (reachable by touch and keyboard, not just hover), a
  "Beginning of the conversation" marker, and Enter/Shift+Enter documented in
  the composer.

Transport, authentication, and the audit trail are unchanged — this is the
same Team Chat, dressed the way the rest of the product dresses.
